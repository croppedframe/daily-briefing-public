# Boston Red Sox Sources

## Prioritize

- Official Red Sox and MLB channels.
- MLB.com Red Sox coverage.
- Established Boston beat reporters and reputable local/national outlets.
- Structured MLB schedule, linescore, probable pitcher, and standings data.

## Use With Caution

- Fan accounts and statistics accounts.
- Rumor aggregators.
- Fantasy baseball accounts.
- Betting, odds, and pick accounts.
- Radio clips or short video snippets without full context.

## Exclude

- Betting picks, parlays, and odds boosts.
- Ticket resale, merch, and giveaway promotions.
- Generic game-thread reactions.
- Unsourced injury or trade speculation.
- Engagement-bait posts and recycled rumors.
