# S&P 500 Market Sources

## Prioritize

- Structured SPY quote and recent daily movement.
- Federal Reserve, BLS, BEA, Treasury, and other primary macro sources.
- Major financial wires and established market outlets.
- Company earnings and guidance only when they affect the index or broad sector tone.

## Use With Caution

- Market-wrap articles that attribute moves without clear evidence.
- Analyst-commentary roundups.
- Finance-media posts built around price action rather than catalysts.
- Social posts, charts, and predictions.

## Exclude

- Signal groups, Discord/Telegram promotions, and stock-pick spam.
- Single-name hype with no S&P 500 implication.
- Crypto-only narratives.
- Generic crash/rally prediction posts.
- Technical-analysis-only posts.
